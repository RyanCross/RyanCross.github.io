Ryan Cross

Software Engineer x Game Developer

Welcome. I'm Ryan and I'm a senior software engineer by day. I've spent the last few years in the HealthCare IT space @ Cerner; Building robust APIs and web applications for a range of healthcare concepts. Recently, I've been fortunate enough to work with a team of extremely talented engineers. Creating services that will help health organizations everywhere share important patient information quicker and with better confidence and trust.

By night, my productive efforts shift towards my long-term dream of building games. Usually working in C# and the Unity engine on fun prototypes or game mechanics I'm trying to reverse engineer. You'll also frequently find me hacking away on absurd ideas at the nearest available Game Jam. Creating that tower defense dating sim you never knew you wanted.

Sometimes I visit the outside world for huu-man interaction. Sometimes in the form of hiking and skiing when the weather permits; but more often you'll find me at the local board game bar, an up-and-coming venue trend I am eternally grateful for.

If your interested in learning more in my industry work, head on over to my resume. For my passion projects (hint, it's mostly games), check out the projects page.
